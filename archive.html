Writing Archive

A collection of writing projects, completed works, and ongoing experiments. Some projects are actively maintained while other represent completed explorations or works in progress that may be revisited.

-

Dark Shadows Episode Reviews
Brief synopsis of the first eleven episodes of the popular soap opera Dark Shadows, which aired on ABC from 1966-1971.

Development Log (old)
Pull updates to your software until they no longer support your device. Here the author discusses issues with adhering to meta.

Femputer Manchine
For ten thousand years the people of planet Earth have been awakening to create civilization and colonize their world. A modern fantasy exploring hidden magical societies and the tension between mundane and mystical realities.

Harmonic Mean
A webnovel exploring mathematical concepts through narrative structure and character development.

Recipes
It so happens you have to eat to live. A collection of cooking experiments and documented recipes, because sustenance is part of the creative process.

Sophie Blue
Sophie Blue was imprisoned for crimes against humanity. She was allowed to keep a diary in her quarters during her time served. A dark exploration of character through the lens of confinement and reflection.

Warcraft
If you play World of Warcraft, you might understand some of this stuff. Stories, character sketches, and creative writing inspired by the World of Warcraft universe.
