## Major Appearances

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Chapter 1: Se souvenir</summary>

Cedric drives the Cavalier east, recounting how he obtained the car in a [less-than-pristine](https://read.nan.nyc/p/se-souvenir?r=72ulkf) state.

</details>

<details markdown="1">

The Cavalier succeeds in conveying its passenger from Newburgh to NYC, but when the transmission [fails catastrophically](https://read.nan.nyc/p/jet-lag?r=72ulkf), Cedric attracts the attention of the police.

</details>
