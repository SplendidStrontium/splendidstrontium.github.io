* cream `¾c sugar` and `½c butter`
* add `2 eggs`
* add `¾c evaporated milk`
* add `2c flour`, `½tsp salt`, `2tsp b.powder`

* fold in `1pt blueberries`
* sprinkle with add'l sugar before baking
* `350°`, 25m or until done
